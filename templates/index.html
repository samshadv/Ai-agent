What is overfitting in machine learning?
Overfitting happens when a model memorizes training data — including noise — and fails to generalize. Think of memorizing answers vs understanding concepts. 🎯
How do I fix it?
Use regularization (L1/L2), dropout, cross-validation, or get more data. Early stopping also helps a lot!

How it works

Simple by design,
powerful by nature

01
You type a message
Your message is captured by the HTML/JS frontend and sent to the Flask backend via a POST request.
02
Flask processes it
Python backend appends your message to the conversation history and prepares the API call.
03
Gemini thinks
Google's Gemini model processes the full conversation and generates a contextual, intelligent response.
04
You get an answer
The reply is returned to the frontend and displayed instantly in the chat UI with timestamps.
